About Page

Noob Text is the fastest™ random paste service on the web. It's built with Go and HTML. That's right. No JS (Technically there is some in the form of HTMX), no meta frameworks, no nothing. Just Go and HTML. It's fast, it's simple, and it's secure. It's also open source, so you can check out the code on GitHub.

Why?

Why not? Why not! Just look! Just look at this beauty

As a guy who likes working on backends and APIs, I wanted to make something that was paradoxically simple and complex. Thing is, in my programming journey as a mostly frontend developer with a little backend as a service (BaaS) experience, I've always wanted to learn the nitty gritty of backend development. Sending the request, storing the cookie, actually implementing a good JWT authentication and more. Well, I did it. I made a backend. And it's fast. And it's simple and it's HTMX powered.
